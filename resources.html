General Tour Information

Customer notifications

When you book a tour with Cycle Arizona, you should receive two notifications via email. The first is a confirmation, which states that your tour is booked, the dates of your tour, and lists all that is included in your package. The second notification should arrive prior to the start of your tour. This will be a reminder and will contain your tour dates and current tour conditions, if applicable. If you do not receive a confirmation within 24 hours, or the reminder notification two weeks out, contact us immediately. Weâ€™ll make sure there are no problems.

Tour checklist

As you prepare for your tour, we want to make sure that you have everything you need to fully enjoy your time in Arizona. Having everything in place when you arrive makes it easy to sit back and enjoy all that your tour has to offer.

  • We recommend the following:
    • Comfortable shoes
    • Hat
    • Comfortable backpack
    • Stainless steel water bottle
    • Multi-purpose tool
    • Insect repellent
    • Sunglasses
    • Sunscreen
